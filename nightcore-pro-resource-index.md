## offer-bridge

- [Should Community Come Before the Course?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/community-before-course-checklist-launch) - Community should come before a course only when ongoing interaction, member support, events, accountability, or shared progress is the core value. If buyers mainly need lessons or files, start with the course or product path instead.
- [Does Your Creator Product Fit a Marketplace Path?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/creator-product-marketplace-fit-checklist-launch) - A marketplace path can help only when buyers already understand the category, the product is easy to compare, and discovery matters more than owning every step. If the buyer problem is still unclear, fix the offer and selling path first.
- [What Should Happen After Someone Buys Your Digital Download?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/digital-download-delivery-after-payment-launch) - After payment, a buyer should know exactly how to access the file, what format they receive, whether updates are included, how support works, and where to go next. If those pieces are unclear, fix delivery before pushing a checkout link.
- [Should Your Freebie Lead Into a Paid Offer Funnel?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/freebie-to-paid-offer-funnel-checklist-launch) - A freebie should lead into a funnel only when it attracts the same buyer who would benefit from the paid offer. Map the opt-in, follow-up, sales page, checkout, and delivery before comparing funnel software.
- [Can a Newsletter Topic Become a Digital Product?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/newsletter-topic-to-digital-product-checklist-launch) - A newsletter topic can become a digital product when readers repeat the same problem, need a reusable asset, and would benefit from a checklist, template, guide, worksheet, or bundle. If trust or clarity is still thin, keep publishing first.
- [Should a Workshop Replay Become a Mini Course?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/workshop-replay-to-mini-course-checklist-launch) - A workshop replay becomes a mini course only when buyers need structure beyond the recording: lessons, modules, access, practice steps, progress, or student communication. If the replay stands alone, simpler delivery may be enough.
- [Can You Sell a Digital Planner With Simple Checkout?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-a-digital-planner-with-simple-checkout-launch) - A digital planner can start with simple checkout when the buyer outcome, planner format, delivery method, update expectation, and support boundary are clear. Use a storefront, marketplace, funnel, or course path only when that path solves the next buyer decision.
- [Spreadsheet Template Checkout Checklist](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/spreadsheet-template-checkout-checklist-launch) - A spreadsheet template can start with simple checkout when the buyer, use case, file format, delivery promise, updates, and support boundary are clear. Use a storefront, marketplace, funnel, or course path only when that path solves a real buyer problem.
- [Should You Build the Course Sales Page or Platform First?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/course-sales-page-or-platform-first-launch) - Build the sales page and checkout path first when the offer still needs validation and the delivery can stay simple. Build the course platform first when lessons, access, modules, and student experience are already central to the product.
- [Do You Need Email Follow-Up After a Digital Product Purchase?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/email-follow-up-after-digital-product-purchase-launch) - Use email follow-up when buyers need onboarding, usage help, access reminders, support boundaries, or a next-step path. Keep delivery simple when the product is self-explanatory and the buyer only needs the file.
- [Is This a Digital Product or a Course?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/product-delivery-vs-course-delivery-launch) - Use product delivery when the buyer mainly needs a file, template, replay, or resource. Use course delivery when the value depends on lessons, sequence, access, practice, and student communication.
- [What Should Be Clear Before You Sell a Template?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/template-delivery-update-checklist-launch) - A template is checkout-ready when the buyer knows what format they receive, how access works, whether updates are included, and what support is or is not part of the purchase.

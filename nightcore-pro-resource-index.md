## offer-bridge

- [What Should Be in Place Before You Choose a Course Platform?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/course-outline-to-platform-checklist-launch) - Compare course platforms after the course outline has a clear student outcome, lesson sequence, access needs, support model, and delivery workflow. If those are fuzzy, keep shaping the course before adding software.
- [When Should a Lead Magnet Point to Checkout?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/lead-magnet-to-checkout-funnel-launch) - A lead magnet should point toward checkout only when it naturally proves the paid problem and the follow-up can explain the next step. If the audience, product, or promise is still unclear, use it to learn before selling.
- [Can You Sell Digital Templates With Simple Checkout?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-digital-templates-with-simple-checkout-launch) - Use simple checkout when the template has a clear buyer, a clear use case, and straightforward delivery. Use marketplace, storefront, funnel, or course paths only when discovery, browsing, explanation, or learning structure is the real problem.
- [Should You Turn a Workshop Into an Online Course?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/turn-workshop-into-online-course-launch) - Turn a workshop into an online course when the value depends on reusable lessons, sequence, access, practice, and student communication. Keep it as a workshop or replay product when one session and simple delivery are enough.
- [Do You Need a Sales Page, Checkout, and Follow-Up Sequence Together?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sales-page-checkout-follow-up-sequence-launch) - Use a connected funnel workflow when the buyer needs explanation before purchase, a clean payment step, delivery after checkout, and follow-up that keeps the promise clear. Use simpler checkout when the offer is already obvious.
- [Sell a Workshop With a Landing Page and Checkout](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-workshop-with-landing-page-checkout-launch) - A workshop usually needs a clear page, payment step, confirmation, access instructions, reminder or follow-up, and support boundary. Use a course platform only when the workshop becomes structured learning with ongoing access.
- [Simple Checkout or an All-in-One Funnel?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/simple-checkout-vs-all-in-one-funnel-launch) - Choose simple checkout when the buyer already understands the product and only needs payment plus delivery. Choose a funnel workflow when the offer needs page copy, checkout, follow-up, delivery, and possibly course or email workflow in one path.
- [Digital Download Delivery Checklist](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/digital-download-delivery-checklist-launch) - A digital download is not ready just because the file exists. The buyer also needs a clear promise, delivery path, update expectation, refund/support boundary, and a checkout surface that fits the product.
- [Can You Sell a PDF With Simple Checkout?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-a-pdf-with-simple-checkout-launch) - A PDF, guide, workbook, or report can often start with simple checkout when the buyer already understands the outcome and delivery is straightforward. Use a larger storefront, funnel, marketplace, or course platform only when the product path actually needs it.
- [Can You Sell a Paid Worksheet or Checklist?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-paid-worksheet-checklist-launch) - A worksheet or checklist can be worth selling when it helps a specific buyer make a decision, complete a task, or avoid a mistake. Keep the selling path lightweight unless the product needs lessons, a funnel, or a full storefront.
- [Do You Need a Landing Page, Checkout, and Email Stack Together?](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/landing-page-checkout-email-stack-launch) - Use an all-in-one workflow when the buyer needs a guided path from page to payment to follow-up. Use a simpler path when the real job is only checkout, publishing, or course delivery.
- [Sell Templates Without Marketplace Confusion: Choose Marketplace, Checkout, or Storefront](https://nightcore-pro.org/go/sell-templates-without-marketplace-confusion-launch) - A template-selling bridge for deciding whether marketplace discovery, owned checkout, a small storefront, or resource-first product work should come next.

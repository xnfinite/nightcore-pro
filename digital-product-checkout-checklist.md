# Digital Product Checkout Checklist

Use this checklist before choosing checkout software for a digital download, template, planner, worksheet, or lightweight product. The goal is to decide what the buyer needs after payment before comparing platforms.

## Quick Answer

A simple checkout path fits when the buyer needs one clear file, a small bundle, or a straightforward template delivery. A storefront, cart, marketplace, funnel, or course path fits only when the buying experience needs more structure.

## Use This When

- The product can be delivered as a file, template, worksheet, planner, calculator, guide, or small bundle.
- The buyer needs clean access after payment more than a full catalog experience.
- You are deciding between direct checkout, storefront, marketplace, or funnel-supported selling.

## Skip This When

- The offer needs lessons, student progress, cohorts, or community access.
- The main need is a landing page plus email follow-up workflow.
- You have not yet defined the file, buyer outcome, update policy, or support boundary.
